What I specialize in

Projects

skinCAM - Present

A direct result of my imagination, skinCAM was spawned in an effort to intertwine the advancing world of machine learning and artificial intelligence in the realm of medical sciences - specifically, in this case, dermatology. skinCAM is available on the Play Store and uses a Convolutional Neural Network trained with 10 skin diseases to provide results with ~95% accuracy.

Grabify - Present

Grabify is an IP-logging utility used by millions on a monthly basis. As a co-developer of Grabify, I helped systematically connect the well-established backend services to the user experience, pulling from the knowledge developed at MySocialHub.

Hypercharged - Present

Built the entirety of hypercharged's website, a site boasting a wide selection of car photos, with a shop in the making in order to make Hypercharged more than just a simple side project.

Breast Cancer Pathway Analysis - Sep. 2018 to Jun. 1

Using my experience with web scraping, I scraped the data from over 900 patients to generate a frequency chart of mutated genes within breast cancer, under the supervision of Dr. Igor Tsigelny. With the scraped genes, several gene "pathways" were elucidated with low P-values, including the ECM-Receptor interaction with a P-value of 1E-12, thereby suggesting that the ECM-Receptor interaction may play a role in breast cancer.

MySocialHub - Present

Assisted in the creation of the largest car community social media platform on the web, known as MySocialHub. As a developer for MySocialHub, I frequently interacted with the MVC Laravel structure, Git workflow, and frontend components that made the platform as popular as it is.

For more projects, please see my GitHub.
